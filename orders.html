Time to press "Order #1" button!

Breakdown of orders

  • Order message - consists of information about your task.

  • Funds - amount of money that will be transferred to your Temporary Balance, if you finish it correctly

If you successfully finish your task - press "Done". In other case, press "Can't be done".

Successful completion of task means - you've granted yourself a certain amount of funds temporary balance.

In case if you accidentally pressed "Done" when the task is not finished - note the administration.

In the end, if you complete the task, you can see in your /info tab, that you've funds in your transactions.
