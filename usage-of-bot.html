Usage of Bot

Information

This is your main tab where you can see all the information about your account in XCOLA Withdraw service.

Information about the worker

  • ID - unique identification number for a user.

  • Role - role of a user in the XCOLA Withdraw service.

  • Name - name of the user.

  • Main Balance (MB) - main balance of a user shows how much money is in the withdrawable pocket.

  • Temporary Balance (TB) - temporary balance of a user shows how much money is in the non-withdrawable pocket.

  • TB to MB transfer time - is used to show how much time is remaining for certain money in TB automatically transfer to MB.

  • You can withdraw money only from Main Balance (MB), twice a day.

  • System automatically transfers Temporary Balance into Main Balance in 2 hours.

  • Time limitation to transfer automation from TB to MB is created in order to keep track of valid orders' completion.

  • You can't withdraw money from Temporary Balance. System by itself will keep track of your Temporary Money.

  • You have nothing to do with Temporary Money.

  • Any frauds in terms of bills and orders will have severe consequences.
