Request MB withdrawal

Requisites to withdrawal of Main Balance

  • You must have more than $3 in your Main Balance (MB)

  • You must have added a payment method in your profile.

Withdrawn money will be sent in 8 hours.

Successful withdrawal will be shown as a cheque in your "Bills" tab.

You can't sent a request, if:

  • You have less than $3 in your balance.

  • You don't have a payment method
